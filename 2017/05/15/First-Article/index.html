##Mutual understanding is the most valuable thing

````It’s widely acknowledged that understanding plays an important role in our daily life.In the dark night of difficulty,understanding makes us take comfort in knowing a bright day will always dawn on us,where hopes and dreams lie.

````Jin Zhiwen knows it well,who is the singer that won the third place in the fierce competition of ‘Voice of China’.However,his success doesn’s come out of nowhere.Just like other young people coming to Beijing pursuing their dreams,Jin once lived in the basement,performed in the bar and sang songs under the overpass.There was even a time when he was drowned in sadness and wanted to give up.It was his girlfriend’s understanding as well as support that made him persevere and achieve success at last.Every time he looks back on those tough days,Jin can’t hold back his tears,which are tears of moving and appreciating.

````As the saying goes,’Understanding from beloved ones is the light tower of your life.giving you warmth and confidence .’Remember to give more understanding to people surrounding you.Only if we are better understood,will our home become more beautiful!
